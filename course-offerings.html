Course Offerings

We offer several courses for both University of South Carolina students and continuing education.

For USC students:

  • "The Fall Flora" (BIOL 526).
  • "The Spring Flora" (BIOL 527).

Both The Fall Flora and The Spring Flora are 4 credits, with two lecture and four laboratory hours per week, and are taught by our Curator, Dr. Herrick Brown.

There is one prerequisite: BIOL 301 (Ecology and Evolution).


Continuing education:

  • "Botany Boot Camp," taught by our Curator Emeritus, Dr. John Nelson.

Please contact us for more information!
