-Additional Resources-

Research projects at the A.C. Moore Herbarium could not be accomplished without the help of its partners and reliable information resources. We hope to help interested individuals access this information by providing a list of other herbaria in South Carolina and links to important sites that we have found useful.

Herbarium Video Library

Thanks to generous support from the South Carolina Midlands Master Gardeners Association, the A. C. Moore Herbarium (USCH) is developing a series of educational outreach videos. Each video is approximately 2 minutes long and covers a different botanical topic. Visit the herbarium's channel on YouTube to view more.
